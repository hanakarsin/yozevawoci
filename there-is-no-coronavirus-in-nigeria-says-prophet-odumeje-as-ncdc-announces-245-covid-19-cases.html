'There Is No Coronavirus In Nigeria' Says Prophet Odumeje As NCDC Announces 245 COVID-19 Cases

Publish date: 2024-05-21

Naija News reports that the controversial Anambra Prophet made this claim on the micro-blogging site, Twitter on Monday, a day the Nigeria Centre for Disease Control (NCDC) confirmed 245 new COVID-19 cases in Nigeria.

The controversial prophet who is Spiritual Director of Mountain of Holy Ghost Intervention Deliverance Ministry World Miracle Center, alias Land of Freedom, insisted that there is no COVID-19in Nigeria, adding that “ if you look deeply you will know that we have only businessmen.

“There’s no #COVIDー19 in #Nigeria, if you look deeply you will know that we have only businessmen,” Prophet Odumeje tweeted on Monday morning before the NCDC announced 245 new Coronavirus cases in Nigeria on Monday evening.

According to the NCDC, of the 245 COVID-19 cases recorded on Monday evening, 76 were detected in Lagos, Nigeria’s commercial city, 37-Katsina 32-Jigawa 23-Kano 19-FCT 18-Borno 10-Edo 9-Bauchi 6-Adamawa 5-Oyo 5-Ogun 1-Ekiti 1-Osun 1-Benue 1-Niger 1-Zamfara.

The NCDC tweeted: “245 new cases of #COVID19; 76-Lagos 37-Katsina 32-Jigawa 23-Kano 19-FCT 18-Borno 10-Edo 9-Bauchi 6-Adamawa 5-Oyo 5-Ogun 1-Ekiti 1-Osun 1-Benue 1-Niger 1-Zamfara 2802 confirmed cases of #COVID19 in Nigeria Discharged: 417 Deaths: 93,” the tweet added.

In a viral video, the controversial Anambra prophet said “On next, first week of April, I come for you Coronavirus in every country in the world, in overseas.

“You will know why I am the end of sickness.” Prophet Odumeje then went ahead to say “I lay curse on China”, adding that he’s coming for India next and that Coronavirus cannot affect him because he is the “virus,” the Prophet had said but was, however, “disgraced” as the virus failed to vanish.
