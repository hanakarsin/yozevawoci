FaZe H1ghSky1 - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-07-06

Professional Fortnite gamer who has gained fame gaming under his H1ghSky1 gamertag. In March 2019, he became the youngest member to sign to the prominent FaZe Clan after rising in popularity as a preeminent partnered Fortnite streamer on Twitch. 

Before Fame

He began posting regularly to his H1ghSky1 Twitch channel in June 2018. 

Trivia

He has seen his Twitch audience grow to more than 700,000 followers. At the time of his signing with FaZe Clan, he became their youngest Fortnite gamer in the history of the crew.

Family Life

He's originally from Romania and later settled in Las Vegas, Nevada. His real name is Patrick Bragaru. He has four siblings, three brothers and a sister. His younger sister Grace appeared in his April 2020 video titled, "MY 5 YEAR OLD Sister DID THIS! FaZe H1ghSky1 Gets PRANKED! *Funny*" His father's name is Petru and his mother's name is Viorica.

Associated With

He joined Turner Tenney on the FaZe Clan's formidable Fortnite gaming roster.
