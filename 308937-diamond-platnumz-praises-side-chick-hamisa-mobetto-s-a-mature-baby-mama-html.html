Diamond Platnumz has described her former mistress Hamisa Mobetto as a mature co-parent months after the two were caught feuding.

"I always ensure my kids are provided for. That is why I do not have any issues with my Tanzanian baby mama Hamisa Mobetto. Though we have had issues in the past, Hamisa is very understanding. I send whatever is needed at the end of every month. But I think Zari has made it difficult because she does not understand why we cannot be together," he said.

He then hinted the Ugandan beauty has made it so hard for him to relate with their two children out of bitterness from their failed relationship.

He was, however, hopeful Zari will come around one day and let him reunite with the little ones.
