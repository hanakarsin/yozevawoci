Jim Iyke Breaks Silence After Publicly Attacking Controversial Actor, Uche Maduagwu

Publish date: 2024-04-07

Nollywood actor, Jim Iyke has taken to his social media to release daring photos of himself hours after attacking controversial actor, Uche Madugwu.

Naija News earlier reported that Jim Iyke had confronted and attacked Uche Madugwu at his place of work, over defaming statements.

Trouble had started a few days after controversial actor Uche Maduagwu made a post via his Instagram questioning the extravagant lifestyle of Jim Iyke.

Uche Maduagwu, in the post, shared on his page, questioned the source of income of his colleague, Jim Iyke, going as far as vowing to quit Nollywood if Jim Iyke opens up on his real source of income. He also called the actor a ritualist.

He threatened to invite the Economic and Financial Crimes Commission, EFCC, to investigate his colleague, citing that the Nigerian people are yet to recover from the shame Hushpuppi brought upon the country.

Sharing photos of JimIyke, he wrote; ‘Please nobody should beg me on this matter because I have already made up my mind on this issue. Is JimIyke the only one in nollywood? Look at the expensive lifestyle e dey LIVE up and down in Lagos and Abuja? Someone told me e no DEY buy expensive clothe from naija again, e now import the most expensive ones abroad, please what is his source of wealth again? Any day we meet or see I go use my Abuja connection to beg EFCC boss to invite am, we never recover from what hushpuppi do us, now this one is changing expensive CARS up and down like Hushpuppi of movie industry, even if dem pay me ten million Naira to enter movie location with am, if e no tell us his source of wealth I gorefuse.’

Reacting to the post, Jim Iyke went over to his place of work, publicly attacking him which stirred a lot of reactions on social media.

The actor, hours after the incident, via his Instagram, shared photos of himself captioning it, ‘If unbothered was a person’
