Kipchumba Murkomen's tweet terming Kang'ata as clown, loser resurfaces

Publish date: 2024-03-14

- Murkomen expressed disappointment in Senator Kanga'ta's leadership in a tweet he posted on August 24, 2020

- The tweet came in the wake of heated debate on revenue sharing formula for counties where the two senators held different views

- Netizens started circulating the post shortly after Murkomen vowed to lead other senators to oppose the removal of Kang'ata as the Senate chief whip

A tweet by outspoken Elegeyo Marakwet Senator Kipchumba Murkomen castigating his Murang'a counterpart Irungu Kang'ata over alleged poor leadership as the Senate majority chief whip has surfaced online.

In the tweet, Murkomen, who has now emerged as Kanga'ta's sympathiser and defender following his unceremonious ouster, described the Murang'a senator as a clown and loser who could not convince anyone.

Netizens started circulating the tweet posted on Agust 20, 2020, shortly after the former majority leader vowed to lead other senators to oppose the removal of Kang'ata as the chief whip.

Unfair treatment

Murkomen argued his Murang'a counterpart was being victimised following his letter to President Uhuru Kenyatta warning him that BBI was unpopular in Mt Kenya and would flop.

He accused Jubilee Party of unfairly targetting his own members and curtailing their freedom of speech and expression.

"We are going to oppose the removal of Senator Kanga'ta as the chief whip when the motion is brought on the floor of the House," he said shortly after the meeting to axe Kang'ata was held at KICC.

The outspoken Elgeyo Marakwet senator also claimed the ruling party had no sufficient numbers to kick out his colleague.

"We are 38 senators that is plus two senators from KANU who were illegally incorporate in the colation. For them to remove Senator Kang'ata as the chief whip, they will need at least 20 senators and I can tell you they don't have those numbers, the maximum they can get is 15

...Kang’ata remains majority whip in the senate. The people running government are now the minority" said Murkomen.

Reactions

Netizens wondered what had changed so fast that the former majority leader had become the chief defender of Kanga'ta, a man he had called out and shamed over his 'poor leadership'.

"Murang'a people left a solid negotiator Senator Kembi (Kitura) and elected a clown called Kang'ata who cannot convince anyone. He should know I am not one of those BBI boys to be blackmailed. How did Jubilee Party replace Senator Kihika (Susan) with such a loser?" read Mukomen's tweet in part.

He was reacting to Senator's Kang'ata's standpoint on the then highly emotive debate on revenue sharing formula for counties.

The Murang'a senator replaced his Nakuru counterpart Susan Kihika as the Senate Majority whip in May 2020 after the latter, together with Murkomen, were accused of disloyalty to the party.
