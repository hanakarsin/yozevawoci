'I Cant Even Lie, My Dm Is Crazy, Cos Every Man Wants Me'

Publish date: 2024-03-09

Curvy Nollywood actress Eniola Badmus has cried out over the number of men flooding her DM and asking for a possible romantic relationship.

Recently, Eniola Badmus has remained one of the most talked about Nollywood stars, especially with her new body transformation.

Recall the curvy actress had gastric surgery in 2021 to extract some of her body fats and was advised to control her food intake.

Since then, Eniola Badmus has continued to churn out her stunning photos on social media causing a stir among many fans and colleagues.

Taking to her Instagram page a few minutes ago, Eniola Badmus shared a lovely photo of herself dazzling in a black dress and beautiful makeup.

Eniola Badmus bragged about being every man’s choice adding that her DM is currently filled with messages from men.

The actress noted that she isn’t lying about people flooding her DM and interested suitors should take things easy with him.

She wrote: “Every man’s choice………..I can’t even lie My Dm is crazy right now y’all should take it easy on me ooooo”.
