What to Know About the Hulu Series

Publish date: 2024-06-27

Ooh La La! Hulu's 'Vanderpump Villa' Invites Viewers to a French Escape

'Vanderpump Villa' premieres Monday, April 1 on Hulu, offering an inside look into Lisa Vanderpump and her staff's daily life at her French villa.

By Haylee Thorson

Mar. 29 2024, Updated 12:30 p.m. ET

The Vanderpump cinematic universe is expanding!

Following the eventful Season 10 reunion of Vanderpump Rules in June 2023, fans of Bravo's long-standing reality series were thrilled to discover that a new installment in the franchise had been given the green light.

According to the streaming service, the show will follow Lisa Vanderpump's staff at her luxurious French villa, as they navigate work and life together while aiming to impress a host of wealthy guests.

Dubbed Vanderpump Villa, the show will be available to watch exclusively on Hulu. Now, let's delve into the details of this latest addition to the Vanderpump saga, exploring everything from episode count to the cast!

What is Hulu's new reality series 'Vanderpump Villa' about?

As previously stated, Vanderpump Villa will center around the staff at Lisa Vanderpump's picturesque French estate, Chateau Rosabelle. While they strive to offer their guests a lavish experience in the South of France, they'll navigate through rivalries, love affairs, and rowdy escapades while living and working together.

In a December 2023 press release, Hulu shared that viewers can expect a whirlwind of events at the chateau, ranging from "firework-filled proposals and opulent events" to unimaginable outings and dramatic confrontations amidst the scenic French countryside. Each day at the Chateau promises a blend of heartfelt moments, hilarious scenarios, and intense encounters involving both the staff and guests.

Throughout the series, Lisa Vanderpump will assess whether this team has what it takes to transform her temporary "pop-up" venture into a permanent fixture and contribute to her burgeoning business empire.

How many episodes is Hulu's 'Vanderpump Villa' expected to have?

Although Vanderpump Rules typically boasts over 15 episodes per season, its Hulu spinoff, Vanderpump Villa, offers a slightly smaller dose. With only 10 episodes planned, akin to The Kardashians, viewers shouldn't assume a decrease in drama intensity. In fact, the success of the 10-episode format speaks volumes, promising riveting narratives ahead!

So, when can Vanderpump Rules fans (and The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills fans, for that matter) expect to watch Hulu's latest endeavor regarding the world of Lisa Vanderpump and her staff?

When is the premiere date for 'Vanderpump Villa' on Hulu?

After the chaotic Season 10 reunion of Vanderpump Rules, details about Lisa's newest reality show, Vanderpump Villa, emerged. Ever since, many diehard viewers have been anxiously awaiting the debut of this series on Hulu, posing the burning question: When will it premiere?

Mark your calendars! On Feb. 21, 2024, Hulu announced that the three-episode premiere of Vanderpump Villa is set to launch on Monday, April 1. New episodes will be released weekly on Mondays on Hulu, culminating in the finale airing on May 20, 2024.

Who is starring in 'Vanderpump Villa'?

In December 2023, the official cast of Vanderpump Villa was revealed! Stephen Alsvig and Gabriella Sanon will take charge as event coordinators. Leading the kitchen are executive chef Anthony Bar and sous chef Caroline Byl.

Marciano Brunette heads the main serving team, aided by Priscila Ferrari and Hannah Fouch, the latter of whom has an on-again, off-again romantic relationship with Marciano.

Andre Mitchell and Terry Hall will craft cocktails as mixologists for the resort. Eric Funderwhite assumes the role of chateau manager, while Grace Cottrell and Emily Kovacs oversee housekeeping duties.

Vanderpump Villa premieres Monday, April 1 on Hulu. New episodes will be released weekly on Mondays.
