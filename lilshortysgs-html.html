Shubble - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-02-13

YouTube gamer who publishes primarily Minecraft content to her channel Shubble, which has earned over 1 million subscribers. She also posts vlogs and pop culture content. In a March 2017 video she addressed the fact that many of her followers think she changes her voice in her videos. 

Before Fame

She took acting classes for two semesters while in high school. She began studying English and communications in college. 

Trivia

She launched her YouTube channel in 2007 but didn't start posting regularly until the summer of 2013. Her shelbygraces Instagram account has over 440,000 followers and has featured mostly selfies. 

Family Life

Her real name is Shelby Grace. She has an older brother and two older sisters. She has dated fellow YouTuber Parker Coppins. She later began a relationship with Lucas Watson.

Associated With

She has collaborated on videos with members of Sky Media, such as SkyDoesMinecraft and Mithzan.
