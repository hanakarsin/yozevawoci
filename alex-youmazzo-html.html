AlexYoumazzo - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-06-21

American social media influencer who has become well-known for her lip sync videos. She has gained over 6 million fans on her Alexyoumazzo TikTok account. She has advertised links for the Shein fashion company website. 

Before Fame

She first posted to her Instagram account in June 2015. 

Trivia

She has lip-synced songs by artists such as Justin Bieber and Megan Thee Stallion. She also received over 87 thousand hearts on a video accompaniment to a song by Doja Cat. She has been known for having a bald head. 

Family Life

She previously dated fellow TikToker Ethan Grey. She has since begun dating Nathalie Vega. Her mom appeared on her TikTok account for Mother's Day in 2019. She has two sisters.

Associated With

She has lip-synced to the song "Used to be" by AJ Mitchell on her TikTok page.
