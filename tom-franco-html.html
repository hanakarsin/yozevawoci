Tom Franco - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-05-27

Unbeknownst to many fans of James and Dave Franco, he is the third and middle Franco brother. He works as an artist and is the founder of the Firehouse Art Collective.

Before Fame

He appeared in the 1990 film Basket Case 2 in the role of Frog Boy.

Trivia

He attended UC Santa Cruz as a fine art major and the California College of the Arts Oakland where he studied ceramics.

Family Life

His mother is Betsy Lou Franco and his father is Doug. In 2014, he married Julia Lazar who was over 22 years older than him. At the time of their wedding Lazar was very sick and the couple married in a hospital solarium. She passed away just under a month later.

Associated With

He runs an art collective in Berkeley and is inspired by the collaborative spirit of Andy Warhol.
