Charles Davis - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-02-01

YouTuber who runs the channel Charles & Alyssa Forever alongside his girlfriend Alyssa Hyde. They are known for posting vlogs as well as pranks and challenges for their 1.9 million subscribers. He previously ran a comedy channel called Dom And Charles. He starred on the MTV show Ex on the Beach in 2018.  

Before Fame

He was born in Indianapolis, Indianapolis; but would later live in Scottsdale, Arizona.

Trivia

He was considered the new guy on his season of Ex on the Beach.

Family Life

He has a sister. He often uploads pictures with his friends and cast mates to his Instagram page. He started dating Alyssa Hyde in 2018, and they run the collaborative YouTube and Instagram channels Charles & Alyssa. 

Associated With

He was pictured with Joe Torgerson to promote Ex on the Beach.
