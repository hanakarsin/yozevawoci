Brittany Nicole Benson - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-02-21

Best recognized as the social media manager of the Snapchat sensation Dr. Miami. She has since become popular on social media herself and launched her BeingBrittanyBenson YouTube channel.

Before Fame

She studied public relations at Florida Southern College and eventually earned a bachelors in communication and media studies at Concordia University, Nebraska. She also worked as an athletic trainer at Florida Gulf Coast University.

Trivia

In addition to her work as a social media manager for Dr. Miami, she has also worked as a HR and Payroll specialist for the Miami Dolphins & Sun Life Stadium.

Family Life

Her father's birthday is in May. She is of Nigerian and Scottish descent.

Associated With

Through her work with Dr. Miami, she has become friends with Raquel Santiago.
