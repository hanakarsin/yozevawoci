UEFA Champions League Matchday 2 (Tuesday, Wednesday) Fixtures, Kick-Off Time

Publish date: 2024-08-09

The 2023-2024 UEFA Champions League group stage campaign resumes tonight after a week’s break and as usual, there are so many nail-biting games to watch out for.

Later tonight, October 3, 2023, Nigerian striker, Victor Osimhen will lead his Italian side, Napoli, to face the most successful team in the UEFA Champions League, Real Madrid at the Stadio Diego Armando Maradona.

The game which will kick off at 8 p.m. is arguably the biggest game to watch out for tonight.

Another interesting Tuesday’s fixture that is worth sleeping late for is the game between Inter Milan and Benfica. The mouthwatering fixture will take place at the prestigious San Siro stadium at 8 p.m.

The biggest fixture on Wednesday, October 4, is the game between Borussia Dortmund and AC Milan at the Signal Idunna Park. The game will kick off at 8 p.m.

The game between Porto and Barcelona, and RB Leipzig vs. Manchester City are also two big games that are worth keeping late for on Wednesday.
