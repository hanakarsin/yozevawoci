Lisa Vanderpump - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-02-03

The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills star who earned a spin-off show with Vanderpump Rules. She is also a celebrated author who wrote a lifestyle book about affordable entertaining.

Before Fame

She once worked as an actress, having appeared on shows like Silk Stalkings with Rob Estes.

Trivia

She continued her reality fame by competing on ABC's Dancing with the Stars in 2013.

Family Life

She married Ken Todd and she has two children, Max and Pandora.

Associated With

She co-starred with Kim Richards in the Bravo series The Real Housewives of Beverley Hills.
