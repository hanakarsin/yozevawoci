Sean McVay - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-04-09

Offensive minded coach who became the youngest hired head coach in the history of the NFL when the Los Angeles Rams put him in charge in 2017. He helped lead the Rams to Super Bowl LIII in 2018-19, where they were defeated by the New England Patriots. He then went on to coach the Rams to victory over the Cincinnati Bengals in Super Bowl LVI in 2021-22. 

Before Fame

He attended the Miami (Ohio) University. His coaching career began in Tampa as an assistant wide receivers coach for the Buccaneers.

Trivia

He was the offensive coordinator for the Washington Redskins from 2014 to 2016 after serving as an assistant tight ends coach and tight ends coach for the organization. 

Family Life

His grandfather is former 49ers GM John McVay. He has a younger brother named Ryan McVay. In 2019, he got engaged to Veronika Khomyn. 

Associated With

He replaced Jeff Fisher as the head coach for the Los Angeles Rams.
