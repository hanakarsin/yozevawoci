Carter Sharer - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-04-17

Content creator on YouTube working alongside his brother Stephen as they experiment with DIY ideas and stunts while also providing daily vlogs. His self-titled channel has earned over 8 million subscribers. 

Before Fame

He has been innovating since he was a young boy. At age 11, he received a welder for his birthday and would become a self-trained, proficient welder. He even made his own engine powered devices including go-carts and hover craft.

Trivia

He graduated from Oakton High School in 2012, and later earned a Bachelor of Science degree from Carnegie Mellon University, where he studied mechatronics, robotics and automation engineering. He was part of the varsity swim team at the school.

Family Life

He started dating fellow YouTuber Lizzy Capri in 2012. They broke up in 2019, but got back together in 2021. He also has a sister named Grace and a dog named Baby Otter Sharer who has his own Instagram account at ottersharer. He is from Oakton, Virginia. 

Associated With

He and the Sharer clan have worked with Shaun McBride.
