February 14 Horoscope and Zodiac

Publish date: 2024-04-07

Uranus is the planetary ruler of the Aquarius, but as you were born in the third Decan, or part, of the sign, Venus lends you some of its planetary power as well. Uranus is the planet of deviation and subsequently, its power is linked to your individuality and freedom seeking qualities. In the same light, Venus's power is reflected in your sociability, appreciation of beauty and cooperative nature. More so than any of the other Aquarius Decans, you have an emotional and sensitive nature. This quality allows you to love whole-heartedly and with passion. In love, your passion may be short-lived however, as you quickly fall in and out of love. In all aspects of your life, restlessness will be an issue, but luckily your warm personality will take you far.
