Louisa Gummer - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-06-12

Model who has signed with IMG Models in New York and appeared in campaigns for Dior. She also has been featured in Vanity Fair and Glamour.

Before Fame

She graduated with a degree in Psychology from Vassar College in 2013.

Trivia

She attended the New York premiere of Suffragette with Mamie Gummer.

Family Life

She's the daughter of actress Meryl Streep and sculptor Don Gummer. She's the youngest of her siblings, singer Henry Wolfe and actresses Mamie and Grace Gummer.

Associated With

She modeled in a campaign for Co-Lab with her sisters Mamie and Grace Gummer.
