Oliver Moy - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-08-06

Social media personality who has more than 11 million fans on TikTok, where he posts lip sync videos of pop songs, often doing duets with other social stars. He also appears on TheMoyBoys Tik Tok channel with his brother.

Before Fame

His initial taste of social media was on Instagram, where he posted his first picture in November of 2015.

Trivia

His Instagram feed is filled with shots that showcase his ability as a photographer to his more than 1.5 million followers. He has a self-titled YouTube channel where he has posted challenges and pranks as well as a range of other videos. 

Family Life

He has brothers named Mateo and Sebastian, the latter of whom was featured in a "MESSY Sibling Tag (Tattoo Edition!!)" YouTube video. His mother is named Natalia. 

Associated With

He posted a picture alongside singer Shawn Mendes to Instagram in February 2018. He was a member of an influencers tour called Socials Tour in 2019 alongside Zoe LaVerne and Cody Orlove, amongst others.
