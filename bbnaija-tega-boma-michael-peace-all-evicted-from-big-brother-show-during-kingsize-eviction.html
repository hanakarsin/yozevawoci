Tega, Boma, Michael, Peace All Evicted From Big Brother Show During 'Kingsize Eviction'

Publish date: 2024-05-03

Four housemates were evicted from the Big Brother Naija reality TV show on Sunday, 5th September in the ongoing season 6 of the show tagged ‘shine ya eye.”

The four housemates evicted were Tega, Boma, Michael, and Peace during the eviction night anchored by the host of the show, Ebuka Obi-Uchendu.

Naija News reports it will be the first time that four housemates are evicted from the show at once.

Tega

Tega was the first housemate to be evicted on Sunday due to the low number of votes from fans during the ‘kingsize eviction’ as tagged by the organizers of the show.

Micheal and Peace were to follow before Boma also got kicked out of the show.

Michael

Peace

Luckily for Nini and Angel, they survived the eviction and get another chance to fight for the final prize at the end of the show.

Boma

However, the night was not only about evictions as there were performances from Arya Starr and Rema to light up the show.

Meanwhile, award-winning Nigerian actress, Tonto Dikeh, on Sunday reacted to the allegations levelled against her by her ex-lover, Prince Kpokpogri.

Recall that the actress’s former lover in a post on Saturday had asked her to come forward with evidence that he cheated.

The Delta State politician, in his post, also commended Tonto Dikeh’s ex-husband, Olakunle Churchill.

Kpokpigri alleged that his relationship with the Nollywood actress which lasted for three months was a living hell and also accused her of sleeping with someone else in Lagos State.

He further stated that Dikeh, after she was caught, begged for a second chance.

Dikeh while reacting to the allegation levelled against her said what the enemy planned for her is blocked by the Almighty.
