Alesia Tess Ortega - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-05-16

TikTok star and content creator who is best known for posting family and lifestyle content with her children such as POV videos, mini vlogs, and lip sync performances. Her alesiatess TikTok account has garnered 250,000 followers. 

Before Fame

She began posting content on Instagram with her children in May 2012. 

Trivia

One of her most viewed TikToks, with over 11 million views, is called "dressing my daughter as me in the 90s."  She posted an Instagram photo from the Fashion Nova beach house in Malibu, California in the summer of 2023. 

Family Life

She is the mother of Josie Alesia, Tessa Ortega, and Nate Ortega. She gave birth to her first daughter in 2003.

Associated With

Her son Nate is dating TikTok star Ayelen.
