Blake Jenner - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-05-31

Best know for playing the the role of Ryder Lynn on the musical drama series Glee and later Adam Foster on the series Supergirl. He starred in the 2016 film Everybody Wants Some!! and the 2018 film American Animals. In 2019, he'd begin starring in Netflix's anthology series What/If.

Before Fame

He started acting professionally when he was nine years old.

Trivia

He was the winner of the second season of The Glee Project, which gave him the opportunity to act on the TV series.

Family Life

He grew up in Miami but moved to Los Angeles in order to pursue acting full-time. In 2015, he married his Supergirl co-star Melissa Benoist; but they filed for divorce after about a year of marriage.

Associated With

He had a guest role on Melissa & Joey, starring Joey Lawrence.
