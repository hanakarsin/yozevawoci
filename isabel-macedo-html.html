Isabel Macedo - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-02-19

Argentinian actress best known for her roles in various telenovelas such as Muñeca Brava, Verano del '98, and Floricienta. She has appeared in over 500 episodes of television throughout her on-screen acting career. In 2014, she gained attention for playing Laura Luna in the TV series Guapas.

Before Fame

She attended University of Belgrano and worked in a hotel before landing her first tole in the telenovela Ricos y Famosos in 1997.

Trivia

She played the role of Cecilia in 2008 film Gigantes de Valdés. She has gained over 2.1 million followers on Instagram for her family and lifestyle content. 

Family Life

She has two younger siblings. She married politician Juan Manuel Urtubey in 2016.

Associated With

She is an Argentinian actress like Mercedes Lambre.
