Ramn Rodrguez - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-03-25

Puerto Rican-born screen actor who appeared in such television series as The Wire and Day Break. He also starred in the films The Taking of Pelham 123 and Need for Speed in 2014. He has amassed over 30 different acting roles throughout his ongoing career. 

Before Fame

He played basketball at Wheeling Jesuit University and later received his undergraduate degree in sports management from New York University.

Trivia

He has played supporting roles in the films Carlito's Way: Rise to Power, Bella, Transformers: Revenge of the Fallen, and Battle: Los Angeles. He played the character Ben Cruz in the TV series The Affair in 2018 and 2019.

Family Life

He was born in Puerto Rico and was raised by his mother in a Manhattan, New York, apartment. He grew up with three sisters and three brothers.

Associated With

He and actress Claire Danes were classmates at the New York City Lab School for Collaborative Studies.
