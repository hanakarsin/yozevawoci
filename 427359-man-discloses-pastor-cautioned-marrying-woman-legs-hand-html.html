Man Discloses Pastor Cautioned Him Against Marrying Woman with No Legs, Hand

Publish date: 2024-08-08
  • Peter Odiwuor talked to TUKO Talks about his journey in tying the knot with his wife who is living with a disability
  • According to the young man, his pastor discouraged him from marrying the woman saying that God cannot reward him with a marriage that will feel like a burden
  • Nonetheless, he kept on loving her and the two wedded and are currently enjoying their honeymoon

A man identified as Peter Odiwuor has opened up about the warnings he got when he decided to marry his lover, whom he met on Facebook.

Peter's love journey

Peter's love for Linet was so strong that even when people discouraged him not to marry her because she had no legs and one hand, he did not listen to them.

The two narrated their love journey to TUKO Talks on the tribulations they faced before eventually saying I Do to each other.

According to Peter, he went to his pastor and even showed him his girlfriend's photos and he was the first to ask him to reconsider his decision to marry the beautiful woman.

"What he told me surpised me. He said Peter you have been faithful to God. And because you have been faithful God cannot reward you because He would not want marital life to look like a burden. This was even after explaining to him about carrying her and returning the wheelchair. He saw that as a burden," he explained.

Their love story began on Facebook and Linet used to like Peter's posts so much that he got intrigued and decided to message her.

He then checked her photos and noticed in all of them she was sitting in a wheelchair and it dawned on him that she was a person living with a disability.
