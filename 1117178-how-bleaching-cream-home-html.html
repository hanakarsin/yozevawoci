Recipe 2

Ingredients

- Turmeric (one pinch);

- Lemon Juice;

- Gram Flour (two teaspoons);

Guide

- Mix the gram flour and turmeric in a bowl;

- Stir the mixture thoroughly;

- Now you can apply the mixture to your body for bleaching.
