Tinubu Arrives Venue Of APC Presidential Screening In Style (Photos)

Publish date: 0001-01-01

2023 presidential aspirant, Asiwaju Bola Tinubu has arrived at the venue of the screening by the All Progressives Congress for politicians seeking the party’s ticket for the 2023 presidency.

Naija News reports Tinubu arrived at the Transcorp Hilton hotel venue for the screening exercise in the company of some supporters including the Lagos State Governor, Babajide Sanwo-Olu and Hon James Faleke.

As earlier reported, the APC screening committee is chaired by former National Chairman of the party, Chief John Oyegun.

See some photos of Tinubu as he arrives for the screening exercise on Monday.

The APC National Leader’s screening is coming at a time the presidential screening committee of the All Progressives Congress has been charged by a member of the party, Sagir Mai Iyali to disqualify Bola Tinubu over alleged certificate forgery.

The petition which was written on the 17th of May, 2022, was submitted on Monday, May 30, and its receipt was acknowledged by one Emmanuel Akpan.

Iyali in the petition forwarded to the chairman of the presidential screening committee, John Odigie-Oyegun said Tinubu never graduated from the University of Chicago.

He added that the former governor lied about his age and state of origin and should therefore be disqualified from the race for the APC ticket.
