What Happened to Rapper Enchanting? She Died at 26

Publish date: 2024-07-04

Rapper Enchanting Died Shortly After Being Placed on Life Support — She Was 26

Gucci Man took to Instagram on June 11 to pay tribute to Enchanting, further confirming the young rapper's death.

By Joseph Allen

Jun. 12 2024, Updated 9:52 a.m. ET

When she was just 22 years old, Channing Nicole Larry, whose rapper name is Enchanting, signed with Gucci Mane's 1017 Record Label. That signing, which happened in 2020, propelled Enchanting to a new level of fame as a rapper. Since then, she continued to gain a following thanks to her presence on social media.

In June 2024, though, rumors began circulating that Enchanting had died following a post from her sister that seemed to suggest she was on life support. Just hours after the news broke, though, rapper CJ Kasino took to Instagram seemingly confirming her death. "They Pulled The Plug Bro This S--t So F--ked Up I Just Knew You was Finna Pull Threw," the rapper wrote on June 11, 2024. So, what exactly happened to Enchanting?

What happened to rapper Enchanting?

Enchanting's sister Kayy Jayy started this rumor with a post on Facebook in which she said that Enchanting was placed on life support. Many misunderstood this post, believing it to mean that she was already dead. Kayy then wrote another Facebook post in which she clarified that her sister was still alive, and was currently fighting for her life. She also added that she was in the hospital with her.

Although Kayy had said that Enchanting was still alive, we don't know exactly what happened to her that led her to be on life support. Sadly, just hours after that update, Kay took to Twitter writing, "I love you girl," followed by a broken heart emoji. Though Kay's post didn't mention her passing, fans immediately interpreted it as such. It was the tribute Gucci Mane's paid to her on June 11 that further confirmed the young rapper's death.

Why was Enchanting on life support?

Following the news that Enchanting was on life support (without any clear explanation), both fans and fellow rappers offered their prayers for her.

“Everybody pray for Enchanting. Pray,” Johnnie Damn D said repeatedly in a video that he seemed to be posting from a hospital.

Since the news of her passing, though, fans have been flooding the comments sections of posts paying tribute to her. "One of the dopest artist that the world didn’t get to fully appreciate," one person wrote under Gucci Mane's post. Under CJ's tribute, one person commented, "rip enchanting, I really liked her music and was looking forward to hearing more, she had a great voice and cadence."

Enchanting was just 26 years old, and some have already started to speculate about what happened to her. Until we know more, including details on her cause of death, it's best to respect her and her family's privacy.
