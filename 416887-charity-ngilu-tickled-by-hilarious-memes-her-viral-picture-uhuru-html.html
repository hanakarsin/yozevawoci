Charity Ngilu Tickled by Hilarious Memes of Her Viral Picture with Uhuru

Publish date: 2024-06-10
  • A photo of Governor Charity Ngilu and President Uhuru Kenyatta seemingly having a conversation lit up the internet
  • Ngilu was amused by the hilarious memes that Kenyans came up with to explain what the duo was possibly talking about
  • The viral picture was taken on Friday, June 25, during the official opening and dedication of the Marian Shrine of Our Lady of Protection, Museve in Kitui central

Kitui governor Charity Ngilu has been tickled by the hilarious memes that Kenyans have made out of her viral picture with President Uhuru Kenyatta.

The head of state and the governor were pictured on Friday, June 25, in a funny pose as they were conversing, and Kenyans, being Kenyans have been making memes trying to explain what the two leaders were possibly talking about.

Ngilu shared the memes on her social media platforms on Sunday, June 27, and asked Kenyans to keep them coming.
