TV anchor Joab Mwaura, wife Nancy Onyancha unveil their media company days after dismissal from K24

Publish date: 2024-02-29

- Joab informed fans he was not yet done with TV even after being shown the door at K24

- His latest move impressed many of his fans who pledged unconditional support

- The journalist and wife Onyancha are among 100 employees who were dismissed from K24 over alleged redundancy

When life gives you lemon, you use the same to make lemonade, and when people throw stones at you, you use the same to build your strong foundation.

That is exactly what revered journalists Joab Mwaura and his lovely wife Nancy Onyancha have done, barely a week after losing their well-paying jobs at K24.

Mwaura on Saturday, June 27, through his official Instagram account announced to fans that he had unveiled a new media company, a venture he will running with his wife.

In his post, the Swahili journalist informed fans that he is not yet done with TV despite being shown the door just recently.

The journalist, famed for his Mgaa Gaa Na Upwa series revealed his new media entity known as Sauti Tajika Productions will be dealing with production of TV/Online commercials, documentaries, voiceover services and events coverage among others.

''I love TV. This is home. I am not yet done, I am in the bathroom. Life is like a camera. Just focus on what is important, capture the good times, develop from the negatives, and if things don’t work out, just take another shot,'' he captioned.

His post impressed many of his followers who thronged the comments section to congratulate him for the great move.

TUKO.co.ke had earlier reported Joab and wife were among over 100 employees who were dismissed from K24 over alleged redundancy.

Their firing moved many people who condemn the media house of pulling such and inhuman act.

Many people pitied Joab and Onyancha since they all had lost their sources of livelihood, considering the fact that they were all fired.
