Shea Marie - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-06-22

Blogger, stylist, and social media influencer known for her Peace Love Shea fashion and lifestyle blog. Her skill as a fashion consultant and model has brought her collaborations with brands like Dolce & Gabbana, Gucci, and Dior. She has also scored appearances in magazines like Glamour, Marie Claire, and Harper's Bazaar.

Before Fame

After attending the University of California San Diego to earn a degree in communications and journalism, she began an internship with a stylist and bartended to pay the bills. It was then that she began Peace Love Shea as a passion project.

Trivia

Also a fashion designer, she launched the debut swimwear collection Same Swim in October of 2015.

Family Life

She grew up in Southern California and has been based in the heart of Los Angeles.

Associated With

She is best friends with singer Caroline Vreeland, the granddaughter of journalist Diana Vreeland.
