Throwback Photos of TB Joshua Capture Moments He Shared Bags of Food to Poor People, Cleaned Church

Publish date: 2024-04-15

- The throwback photos of late TB Joshua have got many people emotional as more praises for his lovely acts poured in

- Many said that the world does not yet know what it has lost until later when people start feeling the absence of the cleric's generosity

- In one of the many photos that surfaced online, the late man of God could be seen transporting bags of food by himself

The world was thrown into great mourning as the popular prophet and overseer of Synagogue Church of all Nations (SCOAN), TB Joshua, died in the early hours of Sunday, June 6.

Tributes and praises for the SCOAN prophet took over social media platforms as people spoke about his numerous acts of generosity.

On the same Sunday, a Facebook user and follower of the late man of God, Prince Toyin Akingbade, shared throwback photos of the prophet that further confirmed what people have been saying about him.

He fed many people

In one of the snaps, the humble prophet carried three backs of food on his head, sweating in his shirt. Another has him giving out foodstuff in abundance to people who needed it.

In a photograph, he took it upon himself to keep the church clean as he got to work, wiping dirt and dust off the chairs.

"No one like him ever in the whole world ..people on Earth will surely miss you I am already missing you my only loved."

The cleric was reported dead on Sunday, May 6.

According to a statement on his Facebook page, the man of God, who passed away just days to his birthday, spent his last moments serving God as he had done his entire life.

"Prophet TB Joshua spoke during the Emmanuel TV Partners Meeting: 'Time for everything – time to come here for prayer and time to return home after the service,""God has taken His servant Prophet TB Joshua home, as it should be by divine will. His last moments on earth were spent in the service of God. This is what he was born for, lived for and died for," the statement indicated.

His family is yet to announce the burial plans.
