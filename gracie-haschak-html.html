Gracie Haschak - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-08-12

Talented dancer who was featured with her sister Madison in the MattyB music video for "My Humps" and his cover video of Kanye West's "Clique." She was also involved as a dancer with the video game Just Dance 2. 

Before Fame

She has close to a decade of training with Temecula Dance Company in the styles of tap, musical theater, jazz, lyrical, ballet and hip hop. 

Trivia

She has been a principal dancer in commercials for Kohls, Disney Tinkerbell, Lalaloopsy and more. She was also a dancer for the shows 90210 and America's Got Talent. 

Family Life

Her three sisters are Madison, Olivia and Sierra. Her dad is John Haschak.

Associated With

She and her sisters did a cover video together of Mark Ronson's "Uptown Funk."
