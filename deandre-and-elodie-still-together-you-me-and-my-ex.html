Are De'Andre and Elodie Still Together on You, Me &amp; My Ex? (EXCLUSIVE CLIP)

Publish date: 2024-04-27

De'Andre and Elodie are a new couple on 'You, Me & My Ex,' but they may not be together anymore after he has some strong words about his ex.

The premise behind TLC's reality series You, Me & My Ex centers on couples where one of the partners is still extremely close to an ex and how the undeniable drama and strife within the newer relationships comes into play. In an exclusive clip ahead of the May 22 episode of You, Me & My Ex, we see evidence of that between De'Andre, his ex Rowan, and his current girlfriend Elodie.

In the clip, Elodie and Rowan have a conversation about just how close De'Andre still is to his ex. Elodie's prying questions make Rowan just a tad uncomfortable, which doesn't go over well with De'Andre. You can't help but wonder if De'Andre's girlfriend is going to be the cause for a breakup rather than his ex.

Are De'Andre and Elodie still together after 'You, Me & My Ex'?

On You, Me & My Ex, Elodie explains that she learned about Rowan and De'Andre's close friendship a few months into dating him. But, because they live in a different state from Rowan, there was never much of a threat. Still, it's hard for Elodie not to totally ignore her concerns when it comes to her boyfriend and his best friend/ex-girlfriend, who also happens to be extremely close to De'Andre's mother.

Outside of the show, neither De'Andre or Elodie have revealed their relationship status on social media. But zero evidence of their relationship working out could mean that there's a strong possibility that the couple is no more. The clip from the May 22 episode certainly makes it seem like a breakup is possible if De'Andre and Elodie can't find some middle ground regarding his ex.

"Honestly, I'm over the same f--king questions over and over and over and over again," De'Andre tells producers in the clip, with Elodie at his side. "That's my best friend. I can talk to her about anything."

When Elodie interrupts to add that she is his best friend, De'Andre clarifies that there's a difference between his friendship with Rowan and his relationship with Elodie.

"No disrespect, but I can find another girl, I can find another chick," he says. "But this is my best friend. I wouldn't give up someone I've known for eight years over a relationship that I've had for eight months."

Elodie then storms away and, it seems, De'Andre may have officially drawn the line in the sand.

On June 12, speculation began brewing about DeAndre's loyalty. After all, we learned that he totally ghosted Elodie ... during her surgery! During that time, he was totally distracted by Rowan. Obviously, Elodie wasn't happy.

It isn't clear yet what this may mean for their relationship, but fans are begging Elodie to let him go.

Rowan insists that she and De'Andre are just friends.

Early in the You, Me & My Ex clip, Elodie asks Rowan personal questions about how close she really is to De'Andre and it doesn't sit well with Rowan. Rowan explains that she wants Elodie to realize and focus on the fact that she and De'Andre are "just friends."

And that may still be the case, but Elodie has a hard time coming to terms with this particular friendship in her boyfriend's life.
