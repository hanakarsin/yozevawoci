Tinubu Mocks Amaechis Show Of Fitness, Reveals Why He's Not Interested In Showing Off

Publish date: 2024-08-16

The former Governor of Lagos State and presidential hopeful, Asiwaju Bola Tinubu has mocked Rotimi Amaechi’s show of fitness during his declaration to run for the 2023 presidential elections.

The national leader of APC, while addressing a gathering in what seemed to be a reply to Amaechi’s action during his presidential declaration said that he is running for presidency, not to become a wrestler.

He said, “Those who think they will see me run around the stadium, I didn’t apply for the job of wrestling.”

After the crowd had erupted in laughter due to his remarks, Tinubu further stated that “What I am applying for has to do with the brain capacity, knowledge, that will make life better for a whole lot of people.’

Recall, that Naija News earlier reported that Amaechi declared his intention to run for the 2023 presidency during a Thanksgiving service organized by the APC.

Before announcing his presidential aspirations the Minister had ran round the stadium.

It was alleged that Amaechi was indirectly mocking the All Progressives Congress, APC, leaders, Abdullahi Adamu, Muhammadu Buhari and Bola Tinubu, who cannot indulge in such physical activity due to their various health challenges and old age.
