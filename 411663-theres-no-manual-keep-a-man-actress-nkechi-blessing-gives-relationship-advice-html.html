Theres No Manual to Keep a Man, Actress Nkechi Blessing Gives Relationship Advice

Publish date: 2024-03-27

- Nollywood actress Nkechi Blessing Sunday has taken to social media, advising netizens about relationships

- The movie star said marriages still crash whether a lady decides to hide or show off her man

- According to the actress, nobody has the manual to keep a man

Popular Nigerian actress Nkechi Blessing recently dished out relationship advice for the umpteenth time on social media.

The movie star who recently found love took to Instagram, saying that nobody has the manual to keep a man.

Nkechi explained that even if a woman decides to show off or hide her man on social media, it does not safeguard their marriage.

The actress advised netizens to find their own "demon" and back it up with prayers. In her words:

“I repeat and I stand to be corrected... We all have our demons we are fighting, find your own demon and back it up with prayers, cus really nobody get manual to keep man...To you reading this, your marriage will work in Jesus name Amen.”
