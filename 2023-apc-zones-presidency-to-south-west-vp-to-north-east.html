APC Zones Presidency To South-West, VP To North-East

Publish date: 2024-07-14

The All Progressive Congress, APC has reportedly agreed on a zoning arrangement for the big six positions in party and government at national level.

According to Tribune, a dependable source informed them that the APC has favored the South-West in producing the next President in Nigeria, while the North-East will produce a running mate.

The senate presidency will be returning to the South-East, while the North-West is scheduled for the Speakership slot.

The arrangement is quite similar to what the Peoples Democratic Party (PDP) in 1999 presented as a governance outlook with Olusegun Obasanjo from the South-West as president and his deputy then, Atiku Abubakar from Adamawa in North-East.

Meanwhile, former President Olusegun Obasanjo has stated that Nigeria has retrogressed under the administration of President Muhammadu Buhari.

According to the former leader, Nigerian efforts to build a nation has undergone significant decline since 2015.
