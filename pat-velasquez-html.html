Pat Velasquez - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-05-09

YouTube vlogger and social media personality best known for her self titled channel. Her videos have been viewed more than 145 million times on the platform. Co-founder of Wagyuniku By Pat and Keng restaurant. She also owns a clothing company Ad Maiora.

Before Fame

She introduced her channel with a June 2020 vlog.

Trivia

Family Life

Associated With
