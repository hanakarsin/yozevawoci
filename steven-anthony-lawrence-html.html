Steven Anthony Lawrence - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-06-28

Former actor best known for his recurring role as Beans on Disney Channel's Even Stevens. His other credits include roles on Married... with Children and The Amanda Show. He has amassed over 30 acting credits throughout his career, which began when he was a kid. 

Before Fame

He began his acting career at age 6 on various television series. 

Trivia

He also guest-starred on shows such as That's So Raven, ER, and Frasier. He appeared in a music video for the rock band Everclear as young version of the band's lead singer. 

Family Life

He had a pet dog named Pooter.

Associated With

He starred on Even Stevens with Shia LaBeouf.
