Babu Owino says he is covered by the blood of Jesus

Publish date: 2024-05-07

- Babu was arrested on Friday, January 17, for shooting a DJ at B Club in Nairobi

- The MP was arraigned on Monday, January 20, and charged with attempted murder after spending a weekend at Kilimani Police Station

- The ex-university student leader was remanded for seven more days pending hearing and determination of his application

- The lawmaker exuded confidence he would overcome the trials since he was covered by the blood of Jesus

Embattled Embakasi East MP Babu Owino has traced his salvation roots days after being arrested and remanded over a shooting incident at B Club in Nairobi.

The outspoken youthful leader was locked up at Kilimani Police Station on Friday, January 17, shortly after pulling a trigger on the club's DJ and spent the entire weekend behind bars.

His tribulations spiked on Monday, January 21, when the court denied him bail and directed that he remains in remand for seven more days pending hearing and determination of his bail application.

Through his Facebook page, Babu said he was "covered by the blood of Jesus" and exuded confidence he would overcome the trying moment.

Shortly after Chief Magistrate Francis Andayi ruled to have him remain in remand, the legislator trooped to his social media sites and called for justice to be served to him just like it would to another person.

He further urged his supporters and Kenyans at large to remain calm and wait for his side of the story once he was out of remand.

"I urge Kenyans to wait for my side of the story of what happened when I come from my new home at Industrial Area prison. I also need justice and it must be served to me. Meanwhile, these are occupational hazards," said Babu.

In another post, the former University of Nairobi (UoN) student leader said he was prepared for any eventuality outlining some of the challenges a devoted political leader must be ready to encounter.

"A politician must always prepare for the following occupational hazards: Must be prepared to serve his people, must be prepared to go to jail, must be prepared to take an asylum, must be prepared to die and must be prepared to enjoy.," he stated.

Prior to the shooting fiasco, Babu had shared another rabble-rousing post hinting ODM leader Raila Odinga would become the country's next president through the "ballot or bullet".

A day later, Babu shot and seriously injured DJ Evolve who he reportedly rushed to Nairobi Hospital.

The DJ has since undergone successful surgery and is responding well to treatment.
