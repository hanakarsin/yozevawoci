Bailey Sarian - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-08-15

Makeup artist and true crime storyteller who shows off variety of looks on her popular Instagram account and YouTube channel. To date, she has attracted more than 2.9 million followers to her Instagram account and over 6 million YouTube subscribers. She has done her makeup and told crime stories simultaneously in many of her videos. 

Before Fame

She graduated from Paloma Valley High School at Menifee, California.

Trivia

She has worked at Sephora and done professional makeup jobs for weddings, commercials and music videos. She can be seen with dramatic tattoo sleeves on both arms. In 2021, she launched a podcast called Dark History that's focused on dark stories from various points in American and world history. It received the Best Podcast award at the 2021 Streamy Awards. She also won the Streamy Award in the beauty category in 2021. 

Family Life

She has an older sister and a younger sister. She was engaged to Fernando Valdez but they split in 2022. 

Associated With

She has done makeup tutorials inspired by celebrities like Kat Von D.
