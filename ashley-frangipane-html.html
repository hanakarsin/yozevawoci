Halsey - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-05-18

Born Ashley Frangipane and better known by her stage name Halsey (an anagram of her first name), she has released notable singles such as "Without Me" and "Bad at Love." They collaborated with The Chainsmokers on the single "Closer" and Benny Blanco and Khalid on the single "Eastside." 

Before Fame

She began her singing career in 2012 when she released a parody of the Taylor Swift song "I Knew You Were Trouble," which she called "The Haylor Song (Trouble Parody)."

Trivia

They won a Billboard Women in Music award for Rising Star in 2016. The following year, she earned her first two Grammy nominations. Their hit single "Without Me" took home the American Music Award for Favorite Pop/Rock Song in 2019. They use both "she" and "they" pronouns. 

Family Life

Her parents are Nicole and Chris Frangipane. They have two younger brothers named Sevian and Dante. She had an on-and-off relationship with her music producer Lido. They dated rapper G-Eazy from 2017 up until late 2018. She then dated Yungblud in 2019 before beginning a relationship with Evan Peters, which ended in 2020. On July 14, 2021, they welcomed their first child, Ender Ridley, with their boyfriend Alev Aydin. The couple separated in April 2023. In September 2023, she began dating Avan Jogia.

Associated With

In April 2019, she was featured in the BTS song "Boy With LUV." The music video for the song broke the record for the most first-day views of a music video on YouTube.
