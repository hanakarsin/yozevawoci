Curtis Harris - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-04-10

Actor best known for his portrayal of Miles Preston on Nickelodeon's Haunted Hathaways.

Before Fame

He made his first TV appearance in a 2009 episode of Grey's Anatomy entitled "I Always Feel Like Somebody's Watchin' Me."

Trivia

He had a small part, along with his brother, in the pilot of Nickelodeon's Sam & Cat in 2013.

Family Life

His younger brother Caleel Harris also became an actor.

Associated With

He and Chico Benymon became two of the leads on Haunted Hathaways.
