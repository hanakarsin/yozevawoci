Burna Boy, Olamide Send Condolences To Davido Over Sons Death

Publish date: 2024-07-17

Popular Nigerian singers, Burna Boy and Olamide Adedeji have joined others to mourn the death of Davido’s son, Ifeanyi Adeleke.

The passing of the late Ifeanyi Adeleke is no longer news and many Nigerians have expressed shock over the drowning accident which took the life of the lad.

Olamide, in a post via his Instastory, penned a short message to Davido as he prayed for divine strength to overcome the painful loss.

He wrote, “I wish you divine strength to overcome this dark times my brother.”

Burna Boy, on the other hand, set aside the music rivalry between him and DMW label boss to mourn Ifeanyi.

In a post via his Instastory, Burna Boy shared a bird and heartbreak emoji.

What Happened When I Spanked My Child In A London Market – Ka3na

Former BBNaija housemate, Ka3na has revealed what happened after she spanked her daughter at a London market.

The reality TV star explained that her daughter suddenly took off into the road while they were at Dalston market, London.

In a bid to correct her child, the mother of one decided to spank her.

However, she was shocked at the reaction of everyone around as they made her feel like she was shooting ‘a blockbuster movie.’

She wrote, “I spanked @lila_bosslady today for running into the road and the whole Dalston market stood still like I was shooting a blockbuster.“

Speaking further, she argued that irrespective of people’s reaction she would still train her child the way she wants.

“See ehn, I must train my child the way my mama train me, this country can’t tell me otherwise,” she added
