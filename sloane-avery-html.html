Sloane Avery - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-06-07

Actress, singer, and model best known for her roles as Patricia in the 2013 indie comedy Don Jon, Ashley in MTV's TTeen Wolf, and Mindy in CBS's Mom.

Before Fame

She briefly became part of the singing group The Rubyz, which was part of the KNECT show iShine, a Christian, teen variety show filmed in Nashville. She played Sparkles on the show for 12 episodes before moving to Los Angeles.

Trivia

Her real, given name is Caitlin Blair Wright.

Family Life

She has an older brother. She grew up in Nashville but moved to Los Angeles to pursue her entertainment career. She is of Christian faith.

Associated With

She had a role in Don Jon, which starred and was directed and written by Joseph Gordon-Levitt.
