Big Love - Cast, Ages, Trivia

Publish date: 2024-06-06

A Mormon family led by Bill Henrickson practices polygamy with his three wives in Utah. Bill Paxton plays the family's patriarch.

Trivia

The show was nominated for eight Emmys including Outsanding Directing for a Drama Series in 2006. Mark Mothersbaugh and David Byrne both composed music for the series.
