Mikaila Murphy - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-03-12

TikTok personality and dance content creator who rose to fame by posting videos on her mikailadancer account. Her dance videos and twerk tutorials have helped her to amass more than 15 million fans.

Before Fame

She began dancing at three years of age and started to take acting classes at ten.

Trivia

She was cast in the movie Gifted Hands.  She had a yes or no Snapchat Spotlight video receive over 400,000 views in 2021.

Family Life

She has a sister named Alyssa and a brother named Andrew. 

Associated With

She appeared in Big Sean's "Single Again" music video.
