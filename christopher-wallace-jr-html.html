Christopher Wallace, Jr. - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-02-10

Son of rapper The Notorious B.I.G. and singer Faith Evans. He is also an actor appearing in the film Notorious as his father at a young age and alongside Will Ferrell in the indie drama Everything Must Go. 

Before Fame

His parents were married on August 4th, 1994, more than two years before his birth. He discovered his interest for acting at age 11.

Trivia

Following in his famous father's footsteps, he is in a rap group called CJ & Jahad alongside his brother Joshua Jahad Russaw. He co-founded a lifestyle brand, which collaborated with Mitchell & Ness to offer unique and vibrant streetwear pieces. He also launched a company with his friend Willie Mack, in 2018, called Think B.I.G., which embraces and advocates for cultural and criminal justice reform.

Family Life

In addition to Joshua, he has sisters named T'yanna and Chyna.

Associated With

His father's longtime collaborator was rapper P. Diddy, also known as Sean Combs.
