US Army recruitment for non-citizens - requirements and application guide in 2020

Publish date: 2024-05-24

The US Army is one of the best integrated and diversified armies in the world. Unlike other major armies, the USA has clear and straightforward policies that streamline US Army recruitment for non-citizens. Every year, the Federal government invites applicants across the globe to join its strong and well-funded military.

Since the infamous 9/11, the US Army has integrated the highest number of non-citizens to the army. Although non-citizens have limitations on deployment and leadership positions, the remunerations are harmonized for all solders —regardless of their citizenship status.

Requirements for foreign recruits

US Federal government spends a huge percentage of its budget on arms and soldiers’ remunerations. Due to the sophistication of its missions and equipment, the military is specific on recruitment. Some of the key requirements to join the marines and other openings include the following:

Physical requirements

One of the first things the army evaluates is your physical qualification. Every potential recruit must go through the Candidate Fitness Assessment (CFA). For one to qualify, one must have the following physical features.

Medical requirements

Medical qualifications are important in the US Army recruitment process. For you to qualify, you must meet the following two thresholds.

  • You must be of good (mental and physical) health.
  • You must pass DODMERB —which is a standardised medical exam.

General requirements

Apart from medical and physical requirements, there are general factors that the US Government evaluates in potential candidates. Some of the general qualifications for US Army recruitment for non-citizens include the following.

  • For immigrants in US military, one must not be legally responsible for any child support.
  • One must not be married.
  • One must not be pregnant.
  • The appropriate entry age to the US Army is 18 years, with a few exceptions.
  • One must also take the ASVAB test. The is one of the key tests that help the US Army in solders’ placements

Application procedure for the US Army

Although the military structure has constantly changed over the years, the US Federal government has retained a simple application process for people willing to enlist into the army. The two-step process includes:

1. Contact a recruitment agency

The US Army has different branches. In the recruitment process, each location has an agency. The importance of the agency is to give you as a potential candidate all the required information about each branch. The agency representatives also answer all the questions you may have about the army.

2. Report to Military Entrance Processing Station (MEPS)

After contacting a recruitment agency, the general assumption is that you have a clear picture of what you want as a potential candidate and which branch in the army is ideal for you. At the MEPS, you must take different tests to weigh your abilities as a person and more importantly, to understand which branch is ideal for you. If the MEPS representatives believe you are a qualified candidate, you take an oath of enlistment.

What should you know about US army recruitment for Africans?

There are two main things to note about US army application for foreigners. They include the following.

Most of the steps are done online

US army is digitalised, and therefore, every step in the recruitment process is done through online platforms. This policy approach makes army jobs for non-citizens visible many people who are interested. Go Army is an ideal place to start the enlistment journey.

In the recruitment process, information is king

The US army recruitment for non-citizens can be complex, especially if you are unsure about the army structure. For example. Marines’ age limit is 28 years while the Navy’s enlistment age limit is 39. Understanding the fine details can help one in making proper decisions.

How to check if the enlisting was successful

After the application and going through MEPS, the next important step in the recruitment process is checking whether your application was successful or not. As earlier mentioned, the recruitment agency keeps most of your information digitally. Apart from information safety and centralising data, the system helps in notifying candidates on their scores and eligibility.

After some weeks, the agency informs the candidates whether the enlistment was successful or not. Previously the government, through the recruitment agencies, used mails. Currently, potential candidates receive notifications through emails.

How can I join the US Army from Kenya?

Kenyan nationals are among the current foreign soldiers in the US Army. For you (as a Kenyan) to join the USA army, you must meet the following requirements.

  • You must have permanent residency status in the USA — primarily through the Green Card lottery.
  • You must be able to speak and write (mainly in English).
  • You must be of good (mental and physical) health.

Can a non-US citizen join the Marines?

Unlike other armies in the world, you do not need to be a US citizen to join the army. However, there are some stipulations. Some of the requirements to join the marines include the following.

  • You must be a permanent US resident for you to qualify.
  • If you live overseas, you must contact the US consulate and apply for permanent residency.

Can you join the military without a green card?

No. You can only join the US military if you have a Green Card. Kenyans are eligible for Green Card and this eligibility bridges the gap for those willing to join the US Army. In the last three years, the rules around the Green Card have been flux. For example, as a Kenyan, you must have a Kenyan passport to meet the entry requirements.
