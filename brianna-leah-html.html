Brianna Leah - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-05-15

Cover artist on YouTube who posts her videos to the channel BriannaCuriel, which has earned over 80,000 subscribers. She is also a songwriter, model, actress and signed dancer. In 2018, she joined the latin girl group Bella Dose.

Before Fame

She began singing in public as early as age 8. 

Trivia

She also enjoys oil painting and fashion design. She is popular on TikTok with over 200,000 fans on her iambriannaleah account. 

Family Life

She has two sisters named Amanda and Dani and a brother named Brandon. 

Associated With

Her rendition of Meghan Trainor's "Lips Are Movin" went viral on YouTube. As a result, she had the opportunity to meet Trainor.
