BBNaija's Emmanuel Opens Op On Being Bi-sexual, Cheating On Liquorose

Publish date: 2024-02-10

Former Big Brother Naija ‘Shine Ya Eye’ Housemate Emmanuel Umoh has opened up about being bi-sexual and allegedly cheating on his colleague and ex-lover Liquorose with a man.

On Saturday, 7th May 2022, an Instagram blogger Cutie Julls made a shocking revelation about the reality TV star and why his relationship with Liquorose crashed.

The report alleged that Emmanuel is bi-sexual, and Liquorose caught him in an uncompromising position with a man during their trip to Dubai in 2021.

Responding to the allegations, Emmanuel, in a post shared on his Instagram page, debunked the report describing it as an embarrassment.

Emmanuel said the report making the rounds online is an attempt to tarnish the reputation that he has painstakingly built.

The reality TV star added that the report has no proof and the blog only aimed to gain cheap publicity and fame with his name.

He said: ” friends, family and fans, I have recently been receiving messages from some misguided people who are making attempts to tarnish my reputation, one I have painstakingly built”.

“These persons peddling this rumour do not have any proof for the allegations they are making, but just to gain cheap publicity and fame”.

“Do not fail for them, they would be hearing from my lawyers and would surely pay for this embarrassment”.
