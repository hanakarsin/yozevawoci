Nigerian Rapper Reportedly Dies Hours After Complaining Of Stomach Pain

Publish date: 2024-05-02

Nigerian tapper Oniyide Azeez, popularly known by his stage name Dablixx Osha, has reportedly died hours after complaining of stomach pain.

The music star whose mother predicted his death, in which he sang in his latest project, had died at the hospital he was rushed to, after complaining of intense stomach pain.

The rapper had only just released his a song, Gone&Dead, before the sad incident.

Mourning his death, popular singer, Mohbad via his verified Instagram, posted the late rapper’s photo with a heartbreak emoji.

Fans and colleagues have taken to social media to mourn his untimely demise.

Mr Ibu’s Daughter, Jasmine Shares Powerful Love Story

Meanwhile, daughter of Nigerian actor, John Okafor better known as Mr Ibu, Jasmine, has shared her love story on how she tied the knot with her US based lover.

It would be recalled that Jasmine Okafor in Februauy  said “Yes” to her lover whom she met on TikTok.

Jasmine had revealed that she met her US-based lover two months ago on the app.

Giving an update about her relationship, Jasmine, in a now-viral video disclosed they later had a low-key wedding.

She also recounted how her online lover turned husband flew all the way from America to propose to her.

She noted that her lover proposed to her in Gambia two months after they met physically.

Jasmine disclosed that they got married 24 days after his proposal and now they are expecting twin girls.
