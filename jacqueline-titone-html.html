Jackie Sandler - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-07-18

Actress who played Martha in Hotel Transylvania, Masseuse in That's My Boy, and had parts in movies such as 50 First Dates. She also appeared in Just Go with It, Grown Ups and Grown Ups 2.

Before Fame

She started modeling in high school.

Trivia

She started her career after meeting Rob Schneider and playing Sally in Deuce Bigalow: Male Gigolo.

Family Life

She married Adam Sandler in 2003 and the couple had two children together: Sunny and Sadie.

Associated With

She appeared alongside Drew Barrymore in the comedy 50 First Dates.
