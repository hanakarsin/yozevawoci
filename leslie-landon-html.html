Leslie Landon - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-03-18

American actress who played the role of Etta Plum on the popular television series Little House on the Prairie from 1982 to 1984.

Before Fame

She started out making small guest appearances on Little House on the Prairie from 1975 to 1981.

Trivia

She earned a Ph.D in psychology from Pepperdine University and has worked with children dealing with loss.

Family Life

She is the daughter of actor Michael Landon and has four children with Brian Matthews, whom she married in 1994.

Associated With

She co-starred with Melissa Gilbert on the television series Little House on the Prairie.
