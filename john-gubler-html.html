John Gubler - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-04-10

Famous as the father of TV actor and model Matthew Gray Gubler. His son has earned fame for his turn as the eccentric Dr. Spencer Reid on the CBS TV series Criminal Minds. 

Before Fame

He earned his law degree from the University of Utah. He subsequently worked as a public defender in 1973.  

Trivia

He has worked as an attorney with his own private practice in the Las Vegas metropolitan area. His son has appeared in the popular film 500 Days of Summer and as the voice of the Riddler in Batman: Assault on Arkham. 

Family Life

His wife is named Marilyn. He has three total children. 

Associated With

His son interned for film director Wes Anderson.
